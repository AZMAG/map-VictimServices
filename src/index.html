Assistance with Orders of Protection
An Order of Protection is a legal restraint used to prohibit a person from committing acts of domestic violence or from contacting people protected by the order.

Civil Legal Advocacy
A service that provides consultation and representation of civil and legal matters.

Criminal Legal Advocacy
A service that provides consultation and representation of civil and legal matters.

Crisis Response
A service provided to prevent, stabilize or resolve an acute or episodic change or anticipated change in a behavioral health condition that may result in the person’s decompensation, relapse, hospitalization, or risk of harm to self or others.

On-Location Counseling
Services provide counseling and professional consultation.

On-Location Financial Assistance
Services that provide some form of money to meet the cost of necessities.

Referrals
A service that identifies and analyzes priority needs, explores options, establishes goals, and develops a course of action that will bring about a desired condition or event at some specific time in the future.
** May include social services, shelter, medical, financial assistance referrals and counseling.

Safety Planning
A service that identifies and analyzes priority needs, explores options, establishes goals, and develops a course of action that will bring about a desired condition or event at some specific time in the future.
